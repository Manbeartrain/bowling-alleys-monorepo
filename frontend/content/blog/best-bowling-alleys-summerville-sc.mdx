## Tips for First-Time Visitors

**Come Prepared:**
- Bowling shoes are required (rentals available at all venues)
- Wear comfortable clothing that allows movement
- Bring socks for hygiene and comfort
- Consider bringing grip aids if needed

**For Families with Kids:**
- Request bumpers for young children
- Ask about ball ramps for very young bowlers
- Use lighter bowling balls (6-8 lbs for kids)
- Take breaks between games to prevent fatigue
- Check out arcade games between bowling sessions

**For Groups:**
- Reserve lanes in advance for parties of 6 or more
- Ask about group packages and discounts
- Consider food and beverage packages
- Arrive 15 minutes early to get settled
- Inquire about private party room availability
