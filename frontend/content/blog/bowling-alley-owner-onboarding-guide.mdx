## Step 1: Claim Your Bowling Alley

Your venue is probably already on BowlingAlleys.io. We've analyzed and listed bowling centers across all 50 states. Here's how to claim yours:

### Finding Your Venue

1. **Go to the homepage**: [BowlingAlleys.io](/)
2. **Search** for your venue by name or city
3. **Click on your venue** to view its page

### Claiming Process

On your venue page, you'll see a **"Claim This Alley"** button if it's unclaimed.

1. **Click "Claim This Alley"**
2. **Sign up or log in** with your email or Google account
3. **Verify you're the owner** (we may ask for basic verification)
4. **Access granted** ‚Äî you're now a verified owner!

Once claimed, you'll see a **green "‚úì Claimed"** badge on your venue page.

## Step 2: Complete Your Venue Profile

Now that you've claimed your venue, it's time to optimize your listing. Head to your **Account page** to edit your venue details.

### What You Can Edit

#### Basic Information
- **Name**: Your official bowling center name
- **Number of Lanes**: Total lane count
- **Description**: Tell visitors what makes your alley special

**Pro Tip:** Write a compelling description that highlights your unique features ‚Äî arcade, bar, cosmic bowling, birthday parties, leagues, etc.

#### Pricing (CRITICAL for SEO)

This is **one of the most important sections**. People search for "bowling prices" constantly.

- **Per Game**: Average cost per game
- **Per Hour**: Average hourly rate per lane
- **Shoe Rental**: Average shoe rental price

**Important:** Enter **average prices**. Help customers know the maximum they'll spend so there are no surprises. If games range from $4-$6, enter $5.

#### Amenities

**Follow the amenities you see on other venues.** We analyze all amenities daily for SEO value and only show those that help customers find you.

#### Contact Information

- **Phone**: Make it easy for customers to call

#### Operating Hours

Enter hours in a readable format:
