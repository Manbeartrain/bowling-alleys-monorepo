# Top Bowling Alleys in North Charleston, SC

Whether you're planning a family night out, organizing a league tournament, or just looking for some casual fun with friends, the North Charleston area offers several excellent bowling destinations. Here are the top bowling alleys in the greater Charleston area.

**Location**: 106 Central Ave, Goose Creek, SC 29445  
**Lanes**: 36  
**Rating**: 5.0/5 (1 review)

Family-friendly entertainment center with 36 bowling lanes, a sports bar, arcade games & billiards. Perfect for large groups and family outings with extensive hours from 9:00 AM to midnight.
