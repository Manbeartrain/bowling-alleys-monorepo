## The Problem We Set Out to Solve

Before BowlingAlleys.io, finding a good bowling alley was harder than it should be.

**Google Maps** shows you locations but not whether the lanes are well-maintained or if they have cosmic bowling on Friday nights.

**Review sites** have scattered information across multiple platforms, most of it outdated.

**Bowling alley websites** (when they exist) often list incorrect hours, vague pricing like "call for rates," and missing amenity details.

The result? Bowlers waste time calling multiple alleys, driving to places that turn out to be closed, or paying more than they expected because pricing wasn't transparent.

We built BowlingAlleys.io to fix this. And at 600 alleys, we're proving it works.

## What We've Learned from 600 Alleys

Building this directory has taught us a lot about America's bowling industry:

### 1. Bowling Is More Regional Than You Think

New England has candlepin and duckpin bowling. The Midwest has historic family-owned centers serving their communities for 70+ years. The West Coast has boutique bowling lounges with craft cocktails. The South has massive entertainment complexes where bowling is one of a dozen activities.

There's no one-size-fits-all "bowling alley"â€”which is exactly why detailed, accurate information matters so much.

### 2. Pricing Varies More Than Location Would Predict

We've found $6/game alleys in expensive metros and $20/hour alleys in small rural towns. The biggest factor isn't geographyâ€”it's **business model**.

Traditional bowling alleys keep prices low and rely on volume and food sales. Entertainment centers charge premium rates for the full "experience." Boutique alleys target date nights with upscale pricing.

Knowing this before you arrive saves money and sets proper expectations.

### 3. Bowling Alley Owners Want to Partner

Since launching, we've connected with hundreds of bowling alley owners who are eager to keep their information accurate and reach more customers.

Many have claimed their listings, uploaded photos, updated pricing, and responded to reviews. Some have joined the Founding Partners program and are actively engaging with their communities.

This reinforced our belief: **there's a real need for a platform that connects bowlers with alleys through transparent, accurate information.**
