# BowlingAlleys.io v1.0.0 - Official Platform Launch

We're thrilled to announce the official launch of **BowlingAlleys.io v1.0.0** - a comprehensive platform built to connect 60 million bowlers with bowling alleys nationwide while providing owners powerful tools to manage and grow their business online.

## üéØ Platform Overview

BowlingAlleys.io has grown from a simple directory to a full-featured platform serving both bowlers and bowling alley owners. With **500+ bowling alleys** indexed nationwide and growing daily, we're becoming the go-to resource for discovering and promoting bowling venues.

## ‚ú® Major Features for Bowling Alley Owners

### 1. **Venue Claiming & Management**
Own or manage a bowling alley? You can now claim your venue listing and take full control:

- **Three Verification Speeds:**
  - ‚ö° **Instant**: Sign up with your venue's website domain email
  - üìß **1-2 days**: Use publicly listed email from your website
  - üîç **5-7 days**: Any other email (manual verification)

- **Self-Service Editing**: Update photos, hours, pricing, amenities, and leagues
- **Founding Partner Badge**: Early adopters get a distinctive purple crown badge
- **Owner Profile Pages**: Create SEO-optimized profiles that interlink with your venues

### 2. **Admin CRM & Outreach System**
Our built-in CRM helps streamline venue outreach and relationship management:

- **Dual Email Templates** (New in v1.0.0):
  - üìß **Cold Email Template**: For initial outreach to unclaimed venues
  - üìß **Follow-up Email Template**: For venues that have already responded
  - One-click copy to clipboard for both templates

- **Outreach Tracker**: Track outreach status, follow-ups, and response dates
- **Status Filtering**: Organize venues by outreach stage
- **Smart Follow-up Scheduling**: Visual indicators for follow-up reminders
- **Bulk Operations**: Manage multiple venues efficiently

### 3. **Advanced SEO & Discoverability**
Your bowling alley gets maximum online visibility:

- **Bot-Aware Meta Tags**: Server-side Open Graph and Twitter Card injection for search engines and social media
- **Dynamic Meta Tags**: Unique titles and descriptions for every venue page
- **City Hub Pages**: Premium city guides showcasing top venues
- **State/Topic Landing Pages**: Hub management system for curated content
- **Sitemap Generation**: Automated XML sitemaps for search engines

### 4. **Pricing Intelligence**
Transparent bowling cost data at national, state, and city levels:

- **Average Pricing Reports**: See how your pricing compares regionally
- **City-Level Cost Pages**: Venue-specific pricing with interactive maps
- **Pricing Badges**: Visual indicators for cost transparency

### 5. **Review System**
Build trust and credibility through authentic reviews:

- **1-5 Star Ratings**: Simple, familiar rating system
- **Real-time Aggregation**: Automatic calculation of average ratings and review counts
- **Edit Your Reviews**: Users can update their reviews anytime
- **One Review Per User Per Venue**: Maintain review integrity
