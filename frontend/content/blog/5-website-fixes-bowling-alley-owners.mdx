## 2. STOP MAKING LOCATION HARD TO FIND

I can't tell you how many sites I've reviewed where it takes me **2-3 minutes to find an address**.

That's insane.

Your address should be in your **header or footer**, easy to copy or click. If your customer can't find you in seconds, they're already walking out the (digital) door.

### Quick Fix:
Add your full address to:
- Your website header (top of every page)
- Your website footer (bottom of every page)
- Make it clickable so it opens in Google Maps
- Include a phone number right next to it

This is especially critical for mobile users who want to tap and navigate immediately.

### Quick Fix:

2. Enter your Facebook page URL
3. Copy the generated code
4. Have your web developer add it to your homepage

Now every Facebook post automatically appears on your website. Zero maintenance required.
