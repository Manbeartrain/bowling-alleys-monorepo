## Tips for First-Time Visitors

**Come Prepared:**
- Bowling shoes are required (rentals available)
- Dress comfortably for movement
- Bring socks for hygiene
- Consider grip aids if you have sweaty hands

**Group Outings:**
- Reserve lanes in advance for parties of 6+
- Ask about group packages and discounts
- Consider cosmic bowling for special occasions
- Inquire about food and beverage packages

**With Kids:**
- Request bumpers for young children
- Use lighter bowling balls (6-8 lbs for kids)
- Take advantage of ramps for very young bowlers
- Check age requirements for different programs
